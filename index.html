We aim to teach fundamentals, grow confidence, and produce a strong team of traders with sound judgement to be leaders instead of followers. We have a mission to be the most trusted name in day trading learning and growth.

Rules



Code of Conduct:

1) Maintain conversation in correct channels (i.e. video game chats in #🎮gaming-chatter🕹 and NSFW content in the #🍸bar-talk🍺 channel). This is important to maintain integrity of the channels to ensure people get correct news when needed for making best decisions and allowing proper DD.

2) Respect everyone and leave ego at the door. There’s no room for any sort of disrespecting of any member based on any difference of opinion or beliefs. Offenders may face punishment up to and including termination from server.

3) Nurture the established culture of caring. We are here as a group of friends and family to help each other learn and grow. Our mission is to ensure growth through learning and learning through group thought.

4) Do not ask for exit and entry points. We can offer advice and strategies as a group but it is your responsibility to do your own due diligence (DD) for your investments. Exit and entry strategies may be discussed but be prepared with your own information and strategies for analysis.

5) Do NOT attempt to make calls without thorough explanation of the reasoning behind your play. Chart analysis, exit/entry strategy, or a new company announcement significantly impacting the company can all be used, but as a group we should not be focused on a hot play that could potentially be a pump and dump that could significantly impact the financial health of our members.

6) No advertising of other servers what so ever. Run it by admin first or auto - BAN
